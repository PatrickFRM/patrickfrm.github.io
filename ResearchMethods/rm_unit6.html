Exercise 6.1

Place these results in the block of cells F23 to F25, using the same format as that employed for the Diet A results in the above example.

Briefly interpret your findings. What do these results tell you about the relative effectiveness of the two weight-reducing diets?

From the results it can be seen that Diet A is more effective at helping weight loss on average as the mean of Diet A is 5.34 compared to Diet B being 3.71 on average showing it being worse for weight loss than A. However the standard deviation is lower for Diet A (2.54) compared to Diet B (2.77).
