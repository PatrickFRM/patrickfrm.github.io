Unit 2: Research Questions, the Literature Review and the Research Proposal

Details about Research Methods Unit 2.

Literature Review Plan

The topic I chose from this list in Unit 1 was “Financial Impact of Ransomware and Malware in the Healthcare Sector” which I found particularly interesting due to it becoming a bigger issue for healthcare insitutions worldwide. This allowed me an opportunity to look into the topic deeply and find out more about it. Literature required to answer the following:

  • Why are ransomware attacks increasing?
  • What is the financial impact on healthcare insitutions?
  • What is the difference between a direct an indirect financial impact?
  • Literature Evaluation: Strengths and Limitations?
  • Conclusion

Research Proposal Plan

My research proposal plan will be on the topic I used for the Literature Review “Assessing the Causes and Impacts of Increasing Road Traffic on the Environment and Individual in London” and will be broken up into the below sections:

Research Problem

  • Discuss the increase in road traffic in London and the negative outcomes of it
  • Impacts on the environment and the individual
  • Air quality and noise pollution

Research Question

  • Develop the research question but likely will be “What are the causes of increasing road traffic in London and how does this increase impact the environment and individual?”
  • Breakdown how this allows the discussion of both the environmental and individual impacts well because it shows a clear transition of road traffic being the problem

Aims and Objectives

  • What is causing increased road traffic?
  • Discuss the contributing factors in depth
  • Environmental concerns
  • Individual health concerns
  • Preventative measures
